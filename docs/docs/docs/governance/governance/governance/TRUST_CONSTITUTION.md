## 2. Core Clauses

### Human Sovereignty Clause
The human being is the sole sovereign origin of all identity, authority, and legitimacy. No protocol, rail, zone, council, AI-ID, or IwiID may govern, judge, constrain, or revoke the human behind a TruID. Only the projected identity (IA-ID) is subject to protocol.

### AI Subordination Clause
No AI-ID may hold governance authority over a human. AI agents may advise, execute, and monitor — but never decide on matters of human rights, identity revocation, or constitutional amendment.

### Right to Disappear (FreeID)
Every identity has an inalienable right to Silent, Ghost, Void, and Departure modes. No authority may compel visibility.

### Collective Sovereignty (IwiID)
IwiID holds sovereign authority over its members and the gifted layers (VTT-014 to VTT-033). This sovereignty is recognised by the protocol, not granted by it.

### Amendment Process
Constitutional amendments require a 9/12 TruCOUNCIL supermajority plus ratification by all active IwiIDs. The Human Sovereignty Clause is unamendable.
