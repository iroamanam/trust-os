## 1. Overview

The AI Family OS is the application layer that transforms the Trust OS into a daily essential for every household. Five integrated modules cover meals, shopping, chores, scheduling, and budgeting — all powered by the Trust Agent, governed by constitutional boundaries, and accessible through a single Parent Dashboard.

### The Weekly Habit Loop

Parents plan meals and generate the shopping list → Kids complete chores and earn BRICKS → The family calendar coordinates everyone → The budget assistant keeps spending on track → Repeat every week.

---

## 2. Core Modules

### AI Meal Planner
Generates a 7-day dinner plan, kids' lunches, and adult work lunches. Respects dietary rules, allergies, cooking time constraints, budget, and pantry inventory. Hands off directly to the Shopping Assistant.

### AI Home Shopping Assistant
Builds optimised shopping lists from the meal plan. Compares real-time prices across Coles, Woolworths, Aldi, Costco, and IGA. Applies split‑basket logic for maximum savings. Exports directly to the retailer's checkout — no payment processing inside the platform.

### AI Chores Engine
Assigns, schedules, and verifies household chores. Rewards completion with BRICK KOIN. Features fair rotation, age‑appropriate tasks, photo verification, and a bonus marketplace for extra tasks.

### AI Family Calendar
The central coordination hub. Auto‑populates meals, chores, school events, shopping deliveries, and family activities. Resolves scheduling conflicts and sends smart notifications to the right person at the right time.

### AI Budget Assistant
Tracks household income and expenses, manages the grocery budget, monitors BRICK KOIN allowances per child, tracks savings goals, predicts upcoming bills, and provides simple weekly spend categorisation — all without accessing bank accounts.
